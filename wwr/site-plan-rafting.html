Overview

Purpose

This site will share stories and experiences with rafting in different locations of the world with my family and friends. This will benefit our rafting company because our site will make it known with our social network.

Audience

Family and friends from age 1 up to no limited age. They speak different languages like Spanish, English, French, Russian, Ukrainian and German. Children are always interested in videogames and books. My Young men and women friends are interested in travelling and to know new places and people. My married friends want to have some fun together with their families. Children need to be listened of their interests like their favorite video games and characters. Young men and women need someone who is able to share their thoughts and dreams. My married friends need to continue raising their children in a healthy and secure enviroment.
